Methodology

How We Build Competence

A structured preparation process designed to build genuine exam intelligence.

01

Choose Your Subject

Log in to your secure dashboard and select any of the available WASSCE subjects. Questions load instantly, filtered by the most recent syllabus.

02

Real-World Contexts

Questions are embedded in authentic Ghanaian scenarios — Mobile Money transactions, ECG utility billing — making abstract concepts instantly applicable.

03

Examiner Feedback

Every answer triggers a full explanation of the working steps and the Chief Examiner's reasoning for each distractor option.

04

Full Data Privacy

All session data is stored entirely within your browser's local storage. No personal data is collected or transmitted — ever.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

Academic Accuracy

Every question is reviewed against the official WAEC 2026 syllabus. Distractor logic is based on documented student errors.

Zero Data Collection

We do not store, track, or profile users. Your practice sessions remain strictly private within your browser.

Immediate Utility

No registration required to begin. Students can access practice questions within seconds of visiting the platform.
