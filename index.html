About the Website

Vision Education is Ghana's premier 2026 WASSCE preparation hub. The platform applies real-world Ghanaian data contexts—like Mobile Money transactions and ECG utility billing—to abstract academic concepts, making high-difficulty questions genuinely accessible to every Senior High School student.

Unlike traditional study platforms, Vision integrates a proprietary AI feedback engine. Every question answered, whether correct or incorrect, triggers a full explanation of the working steps and the Chief Examiner's reasoning to ensure true conceptual mastery.

Core Principles:

  • Academic Accuracy: 100% aligned with the official 2026 WAEC syllabus.
  • Zero Data Collection: All practice sessions remain strictly private and stored completely inside the browser using LocalStorage encryption.
  • Cloud-First Sync: Optional Firebase integration for seamless hand-off between a student's mobile device and the Parent Dashboard.

About the Creator

I am Yaw Ndaase Mensuoh, an education technologist and proud student of St. Augustine's College. I built Vision Education entirely from the ground up, combining my passion for software engineering with a deep desire to help my fellow students succeed globally.

My goal was never just to build another quiz app, but rather to construct a robust, high-performance ecosystem. Over the course of development, I engineered custom UI/UX design systems, implemented a real-time Firestore database for Parent-Student syncing, and integrated AI-driven mock exam features specifically tuned for Ghanaian SHS standards.

When I'm not coding or studying Core Mathematics, I am continuously researching new pedagogical models to integrate into the Vision ecosystem. The ultimate dream is that any student, regardless of background, can have access to a world-class, premium preparation experience.
